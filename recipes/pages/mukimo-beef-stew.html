Mukimo(Mashed potatoes)Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes (russet or Yukon Gold are good choices)
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 to 1 cup milk (adjust to desired consistency)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste

    • Additions:

  • Garlic (minced or roasted)
  • Cream cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Chives or green onions (chopped)

    Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop the potatoes:

    2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized chunks. This will help them cook more evenly.

  2. Boil the potatoes:

    3. Place the potato chunks in a large pot and cover them with cold water. Add a pinch of salt.

    Bring the water to a boil and let the potatoes cook until they are fork-tender (about 15-20 minutes).

  3. Drain the potatoes:

    4. Drain the cooked potatoes in a colander.

  4. Mash the potatoes:

    5. Return the drained potatoes to the pot or transfer them to a mixing bowl.

    Add the butter to the potatoes and mash them using a potato masher or a fork.

  5. Add milk:

    6. Gradually add milk while continuing to mash the potatoes until you reach your desired creamy consistency. You can adjust the amount of milk based on your preference.

  6. Season:

    7. Season the mashed potatoes with salt and pepper to taste. You can also add minced garlic, cream cheese, sour cream, or other seasonings if desired.

    Optional additions:

    If you like, mix in any optional additions like minced garlic, cream cheese, sour cream, or chopped chives.

  7. Serve:

    8. Once the mashed potatoes are smooth and well combined, serve them hot. You can garnish with additional butter or herbs if you like.
