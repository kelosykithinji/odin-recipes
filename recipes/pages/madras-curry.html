Madras Curry

Description

Madras Curry is a famous indian dish that has its origin'sin the Sountern part of India. The curry is a bleand of herbs and spices This curry can be prepared with either vegetables or meat. So you can make a variety of the dish for example; mutton madras curry and chicken madras curry among others. The dish above is madras curry but with beef strips.

Ingredients

  • Madras Curry POwder
  • Cinnamon Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Tale
  • Onion
  • Garlic Paste
  • Ginger Paste
  • Fried Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Tumeric
  • Dried Coriander

Steps

  1. Heat your pan on medium heat
  2. Put your beef strips in the pan and stir till the redness turns brown
  3. Add onion, garlic paste, ginger powder and black pepper respectively
  4. keep stirring until the onions and garlic are cooked
  5. After two minutes add your tomato paste and stir for one minute
  6. Add the rest of the ingriedients and stir
  7. Add small amount of water and simmer the food and leave it cooking for 30 minutes
  8. Keep stiring and ensuring the curry does not dry up
  9. Once ready, serve your curry with rice or bread
